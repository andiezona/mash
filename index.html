Game Rules

The beloved game of MASH (Mansion, Apartment, Shack, House) predicts your future home along with several other important details of your life. Originally played with just a piece of paper and a pen, this version is the virtual fortune telling experience for the modern day!

For each category you will provide a total of 5 responses. To keep things interesting you should give 2 answers you would absolutely want, 1 answer that you are indifferent about, and 2 answers you would absolutely NOT want. If you are playing with friends, let your friends choose some or (if you're brave) all of the answers for you.

When you have completed all of your responses click the "Tell My Future" button at the bottom of the page. This will generate the magic number which will appear on the screen. We will count each category answer, starting from the "M" in MASH, until the magic number is reached - the answer we land on gets crossed off. We continue around the page starting from the previously crossed off answer, skipping over all other crossed off answers, counting up to the magic number again and crossing off responses along the way. When all but one answer has been eliminated from a category that response will be underlined and locked into your future story.

When all categories have been underlined your future has been secured and we will give you the run down of your future life!
